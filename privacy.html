Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Introduction

DreamAi (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our dream‑logging and sleep‑tracking services.

2. Data We Collect

  • Voice Transcripts: Text transcripts of your spoken dreams, stored encrypted in our S3 bucket.
  • Sleep‑Stage Data: Information retrieved from Fitbit (with your permission) about your light, deep, and REM sleep stages.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous metrics (timestamps, feature usage) to improve the app experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate AI‑powered dream summaries and personalized lucidity cues.
  • To display your sleep‑stage progress bar and historical sessions.
  • To troubleshoot issues and enhance app features via aggregated analytics.

4. Data Security

All voice transcripts are encrypted at rest using AES‑256‑GCM. API traffic is protected via HTTPS. We follow best practices to secure your data.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or to comply with a legal process.
  • To trusted service providers (AWS, Fitbit, OpenAI) under strict confidentiality agreements.

6. Your Choices

You can revoke Fitbit access at any time in your Fitbit account settings. You may also request deletion of your dream logs by contacting us.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at privacy@dreamai.example.com.
